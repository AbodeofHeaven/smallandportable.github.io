Saying Hello


Hello

Saying hello is very simple. There are varying degrees of formality.

If you wnat to say hello to someone you know, say xoxu.

This also works for goodbye. If you want to be more formal, say xoxǔʔo.

This actually means "O you! I greet you.".

You may wonder what the ʔ is. This is a "glottal stop", a

Sound that you probably have used before. It is the sound of the hyphen in "uh-oh."


Please and thank you

There are typically no words for "please" or "thank you",

Although you may use the word "uxoxunggo" to say "please".

We will not use this in daily life, however, as it means "I implore you".
