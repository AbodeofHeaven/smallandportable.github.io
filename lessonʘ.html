ʘ


This strange sound is only used in a few natural languages. Due to there being

only two (or three) vowels in ogo, we had to include strange sounds.

This one is prounced by creating a pocket of air with your tounge on the roof of your mouth,

Then Releasing the air by "kissing" your lips. Usually, you can just get away by blowing

a kiss.


It is important not to pause mid-word just to pronounce this.

If you want to hear its pronounciation you can go Here

Try adding the sound mid senstence, so it will sound like any other sound.

If you want to learn how to do this, any Xhosa lesson will teach you how to do it,

Just replace the other clicks with the ʘ.
