Singular Person

I/My mo

You/Your to

He/She/Hers/His no

It/Belonging to it no/ nozogo/ zo (no is preferred)



Plural Person

We/Our mu

Y'all/Y'all's tu

They/Their nu

Those/these nu/zu (not nozogo)


"To be or not to be"

I am mǒgo

This special charcater is a contraction of "oo". Unlike making an "oo" sound as in "too",

It makes a sound like "o'o" empasis on the first O here.

You Are tǒgo

He is/She is/It is nǒgo

InformalIt is zǒgo


We Are mǔgo

This other character is a contraction of "uo". It makes a sound like "wo".

Y'all are tǔgo

They Are nǔgo

InformalIt is zǔgo
