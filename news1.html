April 5, 2018

A declaration of war!


The Governor has released in a statement today that he will staunchly defend all negatve and destructive influences in our region. A vandalism attack has targeted many wiki pages relating to the nation of Iustus, and Iustus has called out to the micronational community to aid them.


Governor Nisu has called this attack "heartbreaking" and "I hope to see more nations rally to Iustus' side".


It turns out that the nation behind these was Magnolia.

The 43rd hopes to not only aid other nations in the

Greater Toronto Area, but also to assert themselves in the World Stage. A declaration was released on the fifth, to "Aid our brothers in Pickering... We may differ on political ideals, but we must do something about this [provocation].


Governor Nisu hopes that Iustus will recognize us as an ally, Just as we have opened our support to them during this critical time.
