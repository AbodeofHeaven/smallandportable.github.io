I say to you:


We shall be governed in this way:


There shall be an administrator, and the first administrator shall be ________

And the administrator's female companion shall be the consort.

He shall be elected from the people, and she shall follow him in the granting of the office.

The two will serve for two years.

There will also be a council, known as the national cluster, whom are all male.

And their job shall be to do the following

Any citizen has the right to bring a tablet forth to the cluster for approval

Those who are in favour of the proposed tablet will debate with their detractors.

The cluster will then vote on the motion.

If it passes, then the tablet will be sent to the administrator for approval.

If he approves, then the consort will have the choice iin approving it as well.

Once everyone approves, the tablet is accepted.

The divisions

The nation shall be divided into divisions, and each of them will have an administrator presiding.

These Administrators may have absolute rule over their division, but any law passed must either recive assent from the Consort or the head-administrator.

The defense

When the nation is under threat, the administrator shall issue a declaration to the public on whether to engage.

If a war is engaged, then the men shall take up sticks and guard the border, and the women and children and government officers shall stay safe behind.

Our codes

The Tablet for criminals shall be the complete list of all the laws.

When a law is passed, the text is inserted into a revised version of the Tablet.


Our borders


The border shall strech from the border of grass on concrete, all the way to the northern fence of the School.

Then It will strech along the railway wall, to the fences of the homes on Golden Avenue.

Then it shall follow the fences along the sides of houses until the borders are closed.

There shall be no surveillance on the border entrances, but the government reserves the right

To remove anyone who conflicts with the laws.
