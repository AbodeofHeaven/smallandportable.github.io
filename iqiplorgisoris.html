THE PARTICLE THEORY OF MATTER



1. All matter is made of particles



2. All particles of one substance are the same (ie the same element)



3. Different substances are made of different particles (ie different elements)



4. Particles are in constant motion, except those at absolute zero (0 K, -273 C)

The more energy each particle contains, the faster it moves.



5. There are attractive forces between particles (forces are stronger when particles are closer together).
